# Kelvin Wambugu LinkedIn Profile Banner Direction

Canvas size: 1584 × 396 px
Platform: LinkedIn profile banner
Brand system: Nuru Digital Marketing
Design intent: minimalist, premium, founder-led, dark futuristic agency aesthetic

## 1. Strategic direction

Position Kelvin as a founder who brings luxury-hospitality discipline into digital growth systems.

The banner should not try to explain everything. It should create an immediate premium impression, then make the positioning clear in one strong line:

- Founder of Nuru Digital
- Chef/luxury hospitality background turned digital strategist
- Websites, SEO, AI automation
- Outcome: trust, visibility, leads

The layout must feel calm, expensive, and uncluttered. Avoid busy icons, long service lists, stock photos, city skylines, fake laptop mockups, and generic "digital marketing expert" language.

## 2. Exact copy

Primary headline:

    From Luxury Hospitality to Digital Growth Systems

Supporting line:

    Websites, SEO & AI automation that build trust, visibility and qualified leads.

Founder line / signature:

    Kelvin Wambugu · Founder, Nuru Digital

Small service chips:

    Web Design    SEO Strategy    AI Automation

Optional micro-label above headline:

    NURU DIGITAL · DUBAI GROWTH STRATEGY

Do not use more copy than this. If the banner feels crowded, remove the service chips before reducing the headline.

## 3. Canva layout blueprint

Coordinate system assumes 1584 × 396 px.

Important LinkedIn safety rule:
- Keep all critical text outside the left profile-photo collision zone.
- Treat x=0–360 and y=190–396 as unsafe because the profile photo/avatar can cover that area depending on device.
- Put the logo in the upper-left only, not lower-left.
- Main message begins around x=500.

### Background

Full canvas rectangle:
- Size: 1584 × 396
- Fill: linear or soft radial dark plum gradient
- Base: #08000E
- Left/deeper plum: #0D0018
- Right glow: #220035

Recommended Canva background build:
1. Add full-size rectangle, fill #08000E.
2. Add a large blurred circle/gradient blob at x=1120, y=-120, size 620 × 620, color #8B12B8, transparency 28–35%.
3. Add smaller blurred circle at x=1280, y=190, size 430 × 430, color #EE2FDC, transparency 18–24%.
4. Add a subtle violet line/glow sweeping from x=720,y=330 to x=1540,y=95 if Canva supports curved line gradients; otherwise skip it. Minimal is better.

### Logo / brand mark

Use Nuru logo asset:
- File: /root/nuru-ai-employees/brand-assets/nuru-digital/nuru-logo-dark.png
- Place: x=72, y=42
- Display size: 168 × 56 px

Place chips below or to the right of founder line, depending on available space.

Recommended placement:
- Start x=503, y=326

- Fill: #220035 with 55–70% opacity
- Stroke: #B08AE0 at 20–30% opacity, 1 px
- Text font: Jost Medium, 15 px

- Internal padding: 18 px left/right

Chips:
1. Web Design
2. SEO Strategy
3. AI Automation

### Right-side minimal visual motif

Use a premium abstract "growth signal" motif instead of literal icons.

Build in Canva:
- Place at x=1230–1465, y=82–300.
- Add 3 thin vertical rounded bars or signal lines:
  - Bar 1: x=1270, y=210, w=7, h=70, color #7055D4, opacity 55%
  - Bar 2: x=1318, y=170, w=7, h=110, color #B08AE0, opacity 60%
  - Bar 3: x=1366, y=120, w=7, h=160, color #EE2FDC, opacity 70%
- Add small glow dots at the top of each bar, diameter 11–14 px.
- Add a very thin diagonal line through the dots, color #B08AE0, opacity 35%.

Meaning: visibility growth, SEO signals, AI automation flow. It stays abstract and premium.

Do not use generic line icons for chef hat, laptop, magnifying glass, robot, rocket, or target. Those will make the banner feel cheap.

## 5. Export notes

Export from Canva as:
- PNG
- Size: 1584 × 396 px
- Compression: none if available
- Transparent background: off

Before uploading to LinkedIn, check:
1. Headline is readable on desktop and mobile.
2. Profile photo does not cover any important copy.
3. Logo is visible but not overpowering.
4. Banner still works if the far right is cropped slightly on mobile.
5. Total message can be understood in under 3 seconds.
